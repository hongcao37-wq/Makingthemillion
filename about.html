About Making The Million

This free tool was created to demonstrate the incredible power of compound interest and consistent long-term investing in the stock market.

Many people underestimate how small, regular contributions can grow into significant wealth over time—thanks to the historical average returns of the S&P 500 (around 10% nominal / 7% real after inflation).

The interactive calculator, stacked growth chart, and key milestones are designed to make this concept visual and motivating. Whether you're just starting out or planning for retirement, seeing your potential "road to a million" can inspire action.

Why This Tool Exists

  • To educate people on the magic of compounding
  • To encourage consistent investing in low-cost index funds
  • To provide a simple, transparent, and educational experience
  • To promote financial independence and long-term thinking

This is an independent project built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Chart.js. No data is collected beyond standard server logs, and everything runs in your browser.

If you find this tool helpful, feel free to share it! Feedback and suggestions are always welcome via GitHub or social media.
